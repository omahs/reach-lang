<span class=\"snip\"><span style=\"color: var(--shiki-token-constant)\">A</span></span> and <span class=\"snip\"><span style=\"color: var(--shiki-token-constant)\">B</span></span> compete to provide computation should provide an opportunity for the first loser to provide their value later, such that it doesn't matter what order they are provided.\n  Mathematically, we could say that the program
