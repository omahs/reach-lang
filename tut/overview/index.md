id=\"over-model\" class=\"refHeader\">Decentralized applications<a aria-hidden=\"true\" tabindex=\"-1\" href=\"#over-model\"><span class=\"icon icon-link\"></span></a></h2>\n<p>\n  <i id=\"p_3\" class=\"pid\"></i>DApps are made of multiple agents interacting with each other through some backend consensus network, like Ethereum or Algorand.\n  These agents act on behalf of principals that provide direction and authority through information.\n  These principals might be humans or other autonomous agents or even committees and organizations with their own structure.\n  The consensus network allows these agents to transfer and receive value in the form of network-specific tokens, like ETH or ALGO.\n  The network also allows the creation of \"contracts\" that ensure that all agents follow the same rules as they take turns computing and publishing values and information.\n  The details of these \"contracts\" are specific to each consensus network, but they are implicitly trusted by all agents and principals because their operation can be independently verified to match the previously agreed-upon rules.<a href=\"#p_3\" class=\"pid\">3</a>\n</p>\n<p><i id=\"p_4\" class=\"pid\"></i>A single Reach program incorporates all aspects of a DApp:<a href=\"#p_4\" class=\"pid\">4</a></p>\n<ul>\n  <li><i id=\"p_5\" class=\"pid\"></i>Participant backends are the agents acting on behalf of the principals.<a href=\"#p_5\" class=\"pid\">5</a></li>\n  <li><i id=\"p_6\" class=\"pid\"></i>Frontends are the technical representation of the interface between the participants and the principals.<a href=\"#p_6\" class=\"pid\">6</a></li>\n  <li><i id=\"p_7\" class=\"pid\"></i>A contract enforces the rules of the program, including the order of operation.<a href=\"#p_7\" class=\"pid\">7</a></li>\n</ul>\n<p>\n  <i id=\"p_8\" class=\"pid\"></i>In Reach, a programmer only needs to specify the actions of participants---what they do individually and what they do in unison.\n  The Reach compiler automatically derives a contract for the consensus network via a connector that enforces these rules.<a href=\"#p_8\" class=\"pid\">8</a>\n</p>\n<h2 id=\"over-minimal\"
